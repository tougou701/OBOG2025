Matsunaga Lab Alumni Reunion

To all alumni of the Matsunaga Laboratory,

I hope this message finds you all well.

Last month, I was honored to receive the 2025 Minister of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology Award for Science and Technology (Research Division). This prestigious award is presented to researchers who have conducted original research or development that holds great promise for contributing to the advancement of science and technology in Japan.

I owe this achievement to the collaborative research we have conducted together over the years. I sincerely thank you all for your support and contributions.

It has been about five years since the Matsunaga Lab relocated from Tokyo University of Science to the University of Tokyo. Due to the impact of COVID-19, we were unable to hold in-person alumni gatherings for the first four years, and it has now been over five years since we last had a chance to meet face-to-face.

To mark this special occasion, I would like to hold an on-site alumni reunion to celebrate this recent award and to reconnect with all of you. We will also hold a symposium as Part 1 of the event, featuring former students from my time at the University of Tokyo as an assistant professor, as well as colleagues and students from Osaka University.

Sachihiro Matsunaga
