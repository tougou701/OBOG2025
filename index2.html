松永研究室OB・OG会のお知らせ -Matsunaga Lab Alumni Reunion-

松永研究室OB・OGの皆様へ

皆さん、元気に過ごしていることと思います。

先月、文部科学大臣より、文部科学大臣表彰・科学技術賞（研究部門）を受賞しました。 このような栄誉に浴することができましたことは、皆さんと一緒に研究したおかげと感謝しています。どうも有難うございます。

松永研が東京理科大学から東京大学に移動してから約5年が経過しました。 コロナ感染症の影響により、オンサイトのOB・OG会が長らく開催できませんでした。 この度の受賞を皆さんで祝うことを兼ねて、オンサイトのOB・OG会を開催します。東大助教時代の教え子、阪大時代の同僚や教え子にも声をかけ、第1部ではシンポジウムを開催しますので楽しんでください。松永幸大

To all alumni of the Matsunaga Laboratory, I hope this message finds you all well. Last month, I was honored to receive the 2025 Minister of Education, Culture, Sports, Science and Technology Award for Science and Technology (Research Division). This prestigious award is presented to researchers who have conducted original research or development that holds great promise for contributing to the advancement of science and technology in Japan. I owe this achievement to the collaborative research we have conducted together over the years. I sincerely thank you all for your support and contributions. It has been about five years since the Matsunaga Lab relocated from Tokyo University of Science to the University of Tokyo. Due to the impact of COVID-19, we were unable to hold in-person alumni gatherings for the first four years, and it has now been over five years since we last had a chance to meet face-to-face. To mark this special occasion, I would like to hold an on-site alumni reunion to celebrate this recent award and to reconnect with all of you. We will also hold a symposium as Part 1 of the event, featuring former students from my time at the University of Tokyo as an assistant professor, as well as colleagues and students from Osaka University. Sachihiro Matsunaga
